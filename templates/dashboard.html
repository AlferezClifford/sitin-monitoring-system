    Laboratory Rules and Regulations

    To avoid embarrassment and maintain camaraderie with your friends and superiors at our laboratories, please observe the following:

    1. Maintain silence, proper decorum, and discipline inside the laboratory. Mobile phones must be set to silent mode.

    2. Games, including computer and card games, are not allowed inside the lab.

    3. Internet use is only permitted with instructor approval. Downloading or installing software is strictly prohibited.

    4. Accessing inappropriate or non-course-related websites is forbidden.

    5. Tampering with system settings, deleting files, or changing configurations is a major offense.

    6. Usage time should be observed carefully. A fifteen-minute allowance is given, after which the unit must be vacated for other users.

    7. Follow these laboratory decorum guidelines:

    • Enter only when an instructor is present.
    • Deposit all bags at the designated area.
    • Follow assigned seating arrangements.
    • Close all programs before leaving.
    • Return chairs to their proper places.

    8. Chewing gum, eating, drinking, smoking, and vandalism are prohibited inside the lab.

    9. Continuous disturbances or inappropriate behavior will result in removal from the lab.

    10. Hostile behavior, including yelling or swearing, will not be tolerated.

    11. Serious offenses will be escalated to the Civil Security Office (CSU).

    12. Report any technical issues immediately to the lab supervisor or instructor.
